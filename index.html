Board Game Projects

Risk Dice Rolling

An interactive visualization tool for dice probability calculations in the classic game of Risk. While playing in an actual game, we started talking about whether the game favors attackers or defenders. This project was inspired by that conversation, and now includes a wide variety of scenarios and visualizations.

Chess Win Probabilities

A sophisticated analysis platform that calculates win probabilities across various chess positions using machine learning. Most similar models are trained on high-level games only, whereas this model accounts for player strength and the increased likelihood that lower-level players blunder.

Academic Publication

Predictive Mathematical Model of Human Lung Mucus

A first-author publication presenting the first-of-its-kind mathematical model that I built to model lung mucus. Also includes results from simulations of the model which reflect the condition of lung mucus under various conditions, including both healthy patients and patients with chronic respiratory conditions such as cystic fibrosis or COPD.
